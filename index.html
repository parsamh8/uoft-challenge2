About Me

I’m a retro designer, web developer and a coding enthusiast with a fascination for how computers work at their core—like a tech nerd who enjoys unraveling the mysteries of processors and circuits. I spent three years as a project coordinator, where I made sure everything ran like clockwork, all while keeping an eye on my cat, PJ, who is basically the boss around here. PJ’s a professional napper and occasional keyboard intruder, but he adds a little extra chaos to my coding sessions.

When I’m not buried in code or wrangling projects, I’ve got a side hobby that keeps things fun: making Spotify playlists. I’m the person who believes there’s a perfect song for every moment, whether it’s late-night coding or just chilling with PJ. If you ever need the right vibe, I’ve probably got a playlist for it! Oh, do you want to know a cool fact? I love the the beatles .
